A Place to Connect

Here is a place where Lego fans near and far can connect with one another. I have always found that Lego is much more fun when we can share our hobby together. There are so many ways to connect with one another. Wether its at a Lego store, one of the many Legoland theme parks, Lego video games, Lego conventions, and even through Lego movies. I'd love to see any of your favorite Lego sets, or original creations! Lets connect and have some fun with Lego!
